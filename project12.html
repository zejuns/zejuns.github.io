Arts and Technology

In George Legrady’s Art and Technology course at UCSB, I explored the intersection of arts and technology through field trip, including exhibitions at UCLA’s Hammer Museum, the Getty Center, and others. For the final project, I presented an analysis of how artistic creation and technical innovation share methodologies and converge at the peak of innovation, showcasing the collaborative potential of these fields in addressing contemporary challenges. One of the projects I referenced in the final presentation, created in collaboration with a friend from SCUT, is currently being submitted for application to a UK university.
