Secetar (Secure Ice Tartar)

Secetar is designed to test the alcohol level of alcoholic drink and whether it contains date rape drugs, aiming to make sure that alcoholic drink is safe for people, especially women. The surface of it could reflect different colors of light depending on the alcohol level. It will sound an audible alarm when date rape drugs are detected.

Winner in Beverage and Food/Catering Supplies, European Product Design Award

Bronze Final Level, G-CROSS Award
