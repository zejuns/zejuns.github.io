These are my undergraduate capstone projects for two majors, each with a distinct focus. The design aspect may differ from the final website. Empowering 3D Creators with Blockchain and NFTs

This project aims to create a seamless bridge between 3D designers, consumers, and 3D printing services through blockchain-based NFTs. Traditional methods of protecting and monetizing digital designs can be complex and limiting, especially for independent creators. By leveraging NFTs, we offer a transparent and secure way to verify ownership, facilitate transactions, and enable new revenue opportunities. Whether selling digital models, licensing designs, or turning them into physical products, this platform provides a dynamic ecosystem for creators to thrive.
